2022-01-03

Sunny. Ate お汁粉 with もち for breakfast. I carried out my day according to the plan I made last night. First I tried to open an account for stock exchange but it had to be put on hold. Second, I ordered tactile switches needed to fix Jizo's sampling machine. Next I washed cloth, myself in the bath, and dishes. Next Mio's leaking 湯たんぽ hot water tank for warmth got soldered w/ the solder and the iron I bought yesterday. An email was sent. Then I climbed on the roof to fix a small 12V solar panel in place. I bent a pair of aluminium strip I picked up in the polytech trash bin, insert one ends to the roof and the panel latches on to the other ends. I lay the wire and started experimenting with lighting options. TODO: make a simple 100V inverter. I ate もち, 雑煮, and ぶり大根 for lunch.　Cabling material was low on stock so I drove to home center in next town to buy it. I bought: 2x 10m enamel wire, 2x 3m 100Φ aluminium duct, A door size polycarbonate board, strong scissors, a tinned hard biscuit, a PET bottle lantern, a たわし tawashi for dish-washing, an ear-muff, a LED light bulb w/ movement sensor, a 3.2Φ drill bit, and a cable cover. I tried to pay with a credit card but it got rejected. I ran to the ATM in a convinience store nearby and got money off the bank and got back to the store. The lady at the cashier helped me carry the things I bought. She asked questions about me. It was warming conversing. I noticed I was high probably from the cbn isolate dissolved into cbd oil + カナムグラ japanese-hop tea I took in the morning + coffee + running. I came home and went for a walk with cindy the cat. At night I sealed the large window on the door in the kitchen with the polycarbonate board for insulation. I ate くまごろう bread and ぶり大根鍋 for dinner. 22:03
